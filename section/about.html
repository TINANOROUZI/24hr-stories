About 24hr Stories

A lightweight, privacy-first way to share moments that don’t need to live forever.

Our take

Social sharing should feel effortless. 24hr Stories is built for spontaneity—quick to post, delightful to view, and clutter-free thanks to automatic expiry. No accounts, no feeds to manage—just moments that fade on their own.

Principles

  • Simplicity: Minimal UI with intuitive gestures and controls.
  • Privacy by default: Stories stay on your device and expire after 24 hours.
  • Performance: Smooth animations and responsive playback on mobile and desktop.
