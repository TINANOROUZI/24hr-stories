How 24hr Stories Work

Create, watch, and move on — your stories disappear automatically after a day.

The flow

  1. Add a story: Click + Add a Story or the + bubble and choose a photo or video.
  2. Full-screen viewer: Clean playback with a progress bar at the top.
  3. Navigate: Swipe on mobile, use arrow buttons, or press ← →.
  4. Pause: Press & hold (or click & hold) to pause the current story.
  5. Auto-expire: Each story lives locally and is removed after 24 hours.
